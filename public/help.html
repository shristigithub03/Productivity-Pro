Help Center

Getting Started

Welcome to Productivity Pro! Here's how to make the most of your task management experience:

How do I add a new task?

Simply type your task in the input field at the top of your to-do list and click "Add Task" or press Enter.

How do I mark a task as complete?

Click the checkmark button () next to any task to mark it as completed.

Tips & Tricks

Boost your productivity with these expert tips:

How can I organize my tasks effectively?

Try grouping similar tasks together or using the priority feature to focus on what matters most.

Can I access my tasks from multiple devices?

Currently, tasks are stored in your browser's local storage. For multi-device access, try our export/import feature in Settings.
